What I do

I'm a Back-end Engineer focused on what happens below the surface. My day-to-day revolves around architecting complex logic, diagnosing performance issues, and refactoring systems that are consuming more hardware than they should.

Most of my experience lives inside FiveM roleplay environments — where real-time simulation at scale isn't optional, it's the product. That means concurrency, state persistence, and server health are constant concerns.

I've also led back-end teams, defined technical roadmaps, and translated community needs into sustainable engineering solutions. When the stack demands it, I navigate React and Vue.js with full autonomy — but back-end is where I'm most at home.
